Design System

By all, for all

Every bit in our world is designed & coded with the primal human desire to connect with one another while speaking the same language. The Design System of Deutsche Bahn aligns with this logic – creating One Single Source of Truth for designers and developers. We embody true collaboration to create consistent, efficient, and user-centric digital solutions – shaping the mobility of the future.

How it works

Our design system is a barrier-breaker, changing the way we design and code products.

  • Accessible

    One place for everyone – accessibility from colors to components is guaranteed.

  • Adaptive

    One token changes it all – from different styles to colors or responsive design.

  • Efficient

    One system does the trick – saving time, money, and nerves for all stakeholders.

Our vision

For the future of mobility, we design excellent digital solutions.

With arms wide open

Truth be told, it wasn't easy. Companies like Deutsche Bahn – running for more than a century – have more walls than meet the eye, separating processes, ideas, and people. We challenged the status quo. No more tossing designs over walls and expecting magic from the developer’s side: to create digital solutions, we work together right from the start. So, what's next?

As railway enthusiasts – or "Superbahner" as we say in German – we don't think internally or nationally. We think European. Just as our trains cross borders into neighboring countries, we operate our design system as open source. In mobility, there are no limits, only the start of new partnerships. Our design system serves as a bridge – by all, for all.
